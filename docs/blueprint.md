# **App Name**: Banglar Voice

## Core Features:

- AI Autonomous News Harvest: A 24/7 scraping engine that ingests viral and breaking news feeds for high-volume publication without manual input.
- Truth-Engine Verification Tool: An anti-hallucination agent where the LLM uses reasoning to cross-reference data and verify facts through the Gemini API.
- Linguistic Style Transformer: Automated professional-grade Bengali rewriting engine ensuring high editorial standards and native fluency.
- Midnight E-Paper Architect: An automated cron-job that compiles the day's top 10 news articles into a professionally designed PDF E-Paper using Puppeteer.
- Voice-Sync Narrative Generator: Synthesizes news content into professional Bengali audio narrations using text-to-speech for accessible media consumption.
- Telegram C&C Webhook: Comprehensive command and control center allowing admins to force sync news or generate E-papers directly through Telegram commands.
- Multi-Platform Dispatcher: Automated publication engine that syncs content across Web PWA, Facebook, Telegram, and video-based YouTube summaries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Deep Royal Violet (#5E41A4) providing a sense of sovereign authority and futuristic news delivery.
- Background: Soft Pearlescent Ink (#FAF9FC) which offers high legibility for long-form news reading.
- Accent: Steel Command Blue (#1B2EA3) used for breaking news badges, active system indicators, and primary call-to-actions.
- Font Recommendation: 'Kalpurush' for an authentic, traditional Bengali editorial aesthetic. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Minimalistic, monochromatic functional icons representing news beats such as national, sports, and AI technology updates.
- Editorial Grid layout featuring a hierarchical feed that prioritizes high 'Viral Score' news with a focus on PWA offline access.
- Fluid micro-transitions for live feed updates and a progressive pulse animation for 'AI Status' health checks.
